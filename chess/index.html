Collection of my Chess Videos

I've been an avid chess player since I learned the rules when I was 5, and started streaming on Twitch in November of 2020. I became a Chess.com Partner in August of 2021, and started creating YouTube videos shortly after that. You can find me under 'bkchess' (Brooklyn Chess) on YouTube, Twitch, and Chess.com. I'm always up for a game!


Some of my accomplishments include leading Northeastern University's A Team to a Division 3 Championship in 2022 as our Board 1, playing on the 2nd Place New York State H.S. Chess Team in 2019, and being in the top 100 American chess players for my age as a 10 and 13 year old!
