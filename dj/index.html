DJ

House and Open-Format Mixing

Electronic Press Kit

I started DJing in November of 2023 and have been hooked ever since. In that time, I've played over 50 sets across more than 20 venues in Boston — including some of the city's top bars, clubs, and even boat cruises. I've performed on a wide range of club-standard gear and spin mainly house music, especially disco or tech house. You can find me DJing under the name 'Juulik'.

Notable Venues:
The Grand Boston, Boston Boat Cruise Summer Series, Loco Fenway (Residency), Scorpion Bar, BSMNT Boston, The Broadway South Boston, The Harp Boston, Mariel Underground, ICON Nightclub, Candibar Boston, Studio B @ BNL, Our House East, Bar Moxy, Fenway Johnnie's

Opening / Supporting Acts For:
Francis Mercier, Jake Shore, Ian Asher, Mashbit, Jake Silva, Goshfather, R3WIRE, DJ Costa, Breakbones
